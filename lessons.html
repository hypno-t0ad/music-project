Major Vs Minor Explained

The Basics

The easiest way to understand what major and minor chords are is to understand this:

Music in a major key 'sounds happy'


Major keys have cheerful and bright melodies like the tune to "Happy Birthday".


Music in a minor key 'sounds sad'


Minor keys have sad and downbeat melodies like the tune to "Greensleeves".

Major Chords

G Major

What is a Major Chord?


A major chord consists of a major third, followed by a minor third

A major chord example is G Major, which is G, B, D.

Minor Chords

A Minor

What is a Minor Chord?


A minor chord consists of a minor third, followed by a major third

A major chord example is C Minor, which is C, D♯, G.
