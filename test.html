How To Play

To play this game click on each play icon and listen to the piece of music that plays. Decide if you think it is a major or a minor chord, then drag and drop it to the appropriate box.

Try and get a perfect score!

Step 1:

Click the play icon to listen to the piece of music

Step 2:

Click and hold on the black background to move the piece to either the happy or sad face

Step 3:

If the piece stays in place, you got it right! Otherwise, try again.
