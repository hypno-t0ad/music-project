Our Mission

Our mission is as follows:

To provide all children with an engaging learning platform in which they can learn about music, music theory, and music in practice

We believe in having different approaches to learning music, so that children and teachers can use approaches that suit them best

We provide educational material, and an interactive game which children can play to test their knowledge!
